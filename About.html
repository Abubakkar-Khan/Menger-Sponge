ABOUT

This website is created as a visual representation of the Menger Sponge, a 3D fractal that showcases the beauty of mathematics and fractals.

The Menger Sponge is fascinating because it illustrates the complexity of fractals, where each smaller part is a reduced copy of the whole structure. The website aims to provide a clear, interactive visualization to help users understand this fractal concept better.

Created by Abubakkar Khan, this project is intended to help students and enthusiasts alike dive deeper into the world of fractals and mathematical visualizations.
